This work proposes an autonomous 3D multi-robot system without external GPS or motion capture systems. The relative position and bearing between any two flying robots are estimated by onboard EKF, which takes as input sensory information (velocity, yaw rate, height, and ranging). With the estimated relative states, experiments are conducted on 5 Crazyflie, including formation flight, and leader-follower coordination while the leader has a monocular camera to detect and fly towards the target.
